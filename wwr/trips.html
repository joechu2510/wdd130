Featured Adventures

The Salmon River Run

Perfect for families and beginners. Enjoy crystal clear water, sandy beaches, and gentle Class II rapids. This full-day trip includes a riverside lunch and plenty of swimming spots.

Los Dientes del Diablo

For the adrenaline junkies! This Class IV adventure takes you through narrow canyons and heart-pounding drops. Professional experience is recommended for this intense journey.

Snake River Overnight

A 2-day immersive experience. Spend your days conquering rapids and your nights under the stars. We provide all camping gear and gourmet meals prepared over an open fire.
